About Us: Team 2

The Visual Question Answering Web-Application for Scene Understanding.

This project is created by three graduate students from Memorial University purchasing their Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence. Our VQA model aims to enable machines to understand, identify, and respond to queries related to visual content based on a particular image. It does this by delivering intelligent systems that can effectively answer questions about images and simultaneously engage users in interacting conversation.
